About Me

Deep in the confines of a dark room is a man sitting at a desk typing at his keyboard. The gentle clacking and patters of keystrokes as he works is all that one can hear. On occasion the man mutters in such quiet jargon that makes no sense to any, but himself.

The pattering then stops and a calm quiet enters the room as the man puts in his final keystrokes. He stares at the screen with a look a satisfaction and leans back stretching in his chair. "Finally done." he exclaims as he puts his head down taking a well deserve peaceful slumber.
